---
title: Legal Basis
description: Required declaration for every lookup — what the values mean and when to use them
---

Every lookup request requires a `legal_basis` field. This is a required declaration of the operational or legal justification for the query. It is recorded immutably in the audit log and included in all PDF report exports.

The value cannot be amended after a query is submitted. Choose the most specific applicable value before submitting.

## Accepted values

### `sanctions_screening`

Use when checking whether an entity appears on a sanctions list, such as OFAC's Specially Designated Nationals (SDN) list, the UN Consolidated Sanctions List, or EU restrictive measures lists.

Applicable for: OFAC compliance programs, correspondent banking, export control screening.

---

### `law_enforcement_investigation`

Use when the query is part of an active law enforcement investigation conducted under appropriate legal authority.

Applicable for: criminal investigations, grand jury proceedings, subpoena-backed inquiries.

---

### `fraud_investigation`

Use when investigating suspected financial fraud, identity fraud, account takeover, or related schemes.

Applicable for: financial crimes units, fraud operations teams, insurance investigation.

---

### `regulatory_compliance`

Use when the query is required to satisfy a regulatory obligation, such as AML/BSA compliance, KYC onboarding requirements, or FINRA/SEC obligations.

Applicable for: AML programs, KYC due diligence, transaction monitoring.

---

### `due_diligence`

Use when conducting pre-transaction or pre-relationship due diligence on a counterparty, vendor, or individual.

Applicable for: M&A target research, vendor vetting, counterparty screening before high-value transactions.

---

### `internal_security`

Use when investigating an internal threat, insider risk, or unauthorized access incident.

Applicable for: corporate security teams, insider threat programs.

---

## How legal basis is used

- **Audit records** — the `legal_basis` value appears in every audit record associated with the query, attributed to the authenticated user or service account.
- **PDF reports** — the declared legal basis is printed on the cover page of every exported report.
- **Filtering** — the `GET /audit` endpoint supports filtering by `legal_basis` to support compliance reporting.

## Important

Selecting an inaccurate `legal_basis` to circumvent access controls or policy restrictions is a misuse of the system and may violate applicable law. Your organization's policies govern which `legal_basis` values are permissible for a given query type.
